Vajra Muay is a martial art which seeks to train the practitioner to use his or her entire body as an attacking surface. In doing this, fundamental striking methods for the fist, open palm, forearm, elbow, shoulder, foot, shin, knee and hip are taught. Employing devastating striking, bone breaking joint locks and throws, this practical martial art is fast, efficient, resolute and deadly.

What does the training entail? What are the benefits of this training?
Basic Stepping and Stance Training. Powerful self defence
Fundamental Whole-Body Strikes (fist, open palm, forearm, elbow, shoulder, foot, shin, knee and hip strikes). Development of a strong fighting spirit
Fundamental joint locking and throwing. The ability to move freely, using the entire body as a weapon
Strengthening and conditioning exercises Confidence and focus
